Carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gas emissions, such as carbon dioxide (CO2), released into the atmosphere as a result of human activities, such as burning fossil fuels for energy, transportation, and deforestation. It is measured in units of CO2 equivalents and is a key contributor to climate change. Managing and mitigating carbon footprint is crucial for addressing climate change and preserving our planet's health.
