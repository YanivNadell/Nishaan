• There are two ways in which you can neutralise your carbon footprint.

 

  • One is by donating to organisations which help to plant trees. Donating to these organisations is the fastest and easiest way to nullify your carbon footprint.

 

  • The other way is to actively monitor your activities and cut down on some activities which significantly increase your carbon footprint. For example, you can walk or cycle to nearby places other than using gas powered vehicles.
